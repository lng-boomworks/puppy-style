## 1. Current State Summary

Puppystyle is a small, community-scale dog business in **La Marina, Alicante (Costa Blanca, Spain)** serving a mixed local-Spanish / English-expat clientele. Primary digital surface is a Facebook page (≈548 likes). No website exists today — this build is the first proper web presence.

- **Existing website**: none
- **Social channels covered**: Facebook (primary). No visible Instagram/Google Business presence surfaced in search.
- **Google Business Profile**: not found in search results (gap worth closing alongside the site)
- **Overall digital maturity**: **LOW**

### Observations

- Listed by third-party directories as **peluquería canina + boutique + guardería canina** — three distinct revenue streams (grooming, retail, daycare) that a single-service site would underplay.
- La Marina sits inside a heavily Anglophone expat zone — bilingual EN/ES is the category norm (direct neighbour Pet Center Patricia runs EN/ES/DE/NL/FR).
- Facebook-only presence means customers can't browse services/prices outside logged-in FB — this is the #1 friction point.

### Weaknesses

- No web storefront → no inbound SEO, no shareable service list, no booking path.
- No surfaced reviews aggregator → trust has to be rebuilt each time.
- No visible team/before-&-after content indexable by Google.

### Opportunities

- Own the terms "dog grooming La Marina" and "dog daycare La Marina" — minimal web competition locally.
- Convert the FB audience (548+) into web/bookings traffic with a single link-in-bio.
- Stand out through **fun, dog-led brand tone** — most local competitors lean clinical/vet-adjacent.

## 4. Opportunities to Outperform

- **Opportunity 1** — Own the "fun & dog-friendly" brand lane locally
  - _Grounded in_: Every surfaced competitor in the Alicante/La Marina area reads clinical or utilitarian. A playful-but-professional tone is unoccupied. User brief explicitly requested "fun, dog-friendly."
- **Opportunity 2** — Lead with a real before-&-after gallery + named team
  - _Grounded in_: Pretty Pawz, Wet Dog London, and Pet Center Patricia all skip this. It's the single biggest trust lift available at near-zero cost.
- **Opportunity 3** — Publish transparent size-tier pricing (S/M/L/XL)
  - _Grounded in_: Wet Dog London proves it works; no Costa Blanca local does it. Category complaint: "no prices until you arrive."
- **Opportunity 4** — Bilingual EN/ES from day one, with WhatsApp-first booking
  - _Grounded in_: Expat market norm (Pet Center Patricia runs 5 languages). WhatsApp is the de-facto Spanish booking channel.
- **Opportunity 5** — Surface the **three revenue streams** (grooming + boutique + daycare) as equal pillars
  - _Grounded in_: Third-party directories already describe Puppystyle as all three — the website should match that breadth instead of collapsing it to "grooming salon."

## 5. Content Direction

- **Tone of voice**: playful, dog-led, warm — "the salon where your pup actually wants to go." Light humour, not precious. Think _kids-party energy_ rather than _vet clinic energy_.
- **Reading level**: everyday English / everyday Spanish — short sentences, no jargon.
- **Core topics / service framing**: three pillars — **Grooming**, **Daycare**, **Boutique**. Each pillar gets its own section on Home and its own moment on the Services page.
- **Trust signals to prioritise**: team faces + names, before/after photo strip, Google/Facebook review count, WhatsApp number, La Marina address with map, bilingual badge.
- **Hero headline direction**: tail-wagging one-liner that signals fun + expertise. Options to test:
  - _"The grooming salon your dog will drag you to."_
  - _"Wag-worthy grooms, sleepovers & treats in La Marina."_
  - _"Pups in. Smiles out."_
- **Primary CTA direction**: **"Book on WhatsApp"** (primary) + **"See the salon"** (secondary, scrolls to gallery).

## 6. Differentiation Strategy

**One-line differentiator**: The only dog spa in La Marina that feels like a playground — grooming, daycare and boutique under one tail-waggingly fun roof.

### Proof points

1. Three services, one door — grooming + daycare + boutique (most competitors do one or two).
2. Real team, real pups — named groomers + before/after gallery that competitors avoid.
3. Price-clear, bilingual, WhatsApp-first — friction removed for the Costa Blanca expat + local market.

### Messaging architecture

- **Headline idea**: _"The grooming salon your dog will drag you to."_
- **Sub-headline idea**: _"Grooming, daycare & treats in La Marina — cheerful humans, happy pups, wag-worthy results."_
- **Three-word brand essence**: **Playful · Caring · Local**
